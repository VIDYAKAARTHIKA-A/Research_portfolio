### 🔬 Research Exploration Timeline

**2024 – Present**  
**Broad Exploration across Generative AI, Multimodal Systems, and Robust Representation Learning**

- **Generative Adversarial Networks (GANs) & Image Synthesis**  
  Conducted an in-depth comparative study of **25+ GAN architectures** (DCGAN, CycleGAN, WGAN, Pix2Pix, Progressive GAN, SRGAN, ESRGAN, DeblurGAN, and U-Net variants). Analyzed training stability, mode collapse, latent representation quality, perceptual losses, and convergence behavior.  
  Developed a **GAN-based aerial image enhancement and stitching pipeline** that improved PSNR from **15 dB to 27 dB** for drone surveillance imagery, significantly enhancing feature matching robustness and reconstruction quality under challenging conditions.

- **Image Stitching & Geometric Alignment**  
  Explored classical and learning-based pipelines for aerial/surveillance imagery, mastering homography estimation, multi-view registration, and advanced warping techniques (perspective, cylindrical, affine, and mesh-based). Focused on distortion reduction, seam consistency, and robustness across varying viewpoints and lighting.

- **Multimodal Assistive AI — VisionMate**  
  Designed and implemented an end-to-end **multimodal assistive system** for visually impaired users. Integrated **YOLOv8** for real-time object detection and **BLIP** for contextual image captioning, coupled with audio narration pipelines. Built a mobile-friendly inference system with Flask and Kivy for hands-free scene understanding and navigation support.

- **Explainable Medical Audio Intelligence — EchoVitals**  
  Developed an AI-powered diagnostic system using fine-tuned **Audio Spectrogram Transformer (AST)**, achieving **92% diagnostic accuracy** for respiratory and voice disorders.  
  Integrated state-of-the-art interpretability techniques — **LIME, SHAP, Integrated Gradients**, and attention visualization — to enhance clinical transparency and trust. Recognized at the **Bit ’n’ Build Hackathon 2025** for diagnostic precision and explainability.

- **Noise-Robust Representation Learning for Medical Imaging** *(Ongoing Core Research)*  
  Leading research on robust feature learning under noisy labels, severe class imbalance, weak supervision, and domain shifts in medical datasets.  
  Developing **self-supervised contrastive learning** frameworks combined with noise-aware optimization strategies using Gaussian Mixture Models (GMM), curriculum learning, and Symmetric Cross Entropy loss. Actively benchmarking against SOTA methods to improve generalization in safety-critical clinical settings.

**2025 Internships – Expanding Breadth into Applied AI & Agentic Systems**

  Built a **multimodal fake application detection framework** using LangChain-based agents, integrating logo verification, developer profiling, phishing detection, and anomaly analysis.  
  Improved multi-class webpage classification accuracy from **86% to 93%** and reduced false positives by **60%** through adversarial evaluation and structured prompt optimization.

- **Summer Research Intern, DataHub, IIT Kharagpur** (May – Jul 2025)
